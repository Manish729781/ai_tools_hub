About ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a state-of-the-art conversational AI developed by OpenAI, designed to understand and generate human-like text responses. Built on the GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) architecture, it excels at a wide range of tasks including writing, coding, analysis, and creative problem-solving.

The tool has revolutionized how people interact with AI, offering natural language conversations that feel intuitive and helpful. Whether you're a student, professional, developer, or creative, ChatGPT adapts to your needs and communication style.

With continuous updates and improvements, ChatGPT remains at the forefront of AI technology, offering both free and premium tiers to accommodate different user requirements and use cases.

import openai

openai.api_key = "your-api-key"

  model="gpt-3.5-turbo",

  ]

print(response.choices[0].message.content)
